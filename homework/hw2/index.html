Music Player Guide

  • Press W A S D to move the crane.
  • Hold 1 2 3 with R (clockwise) F (counter-clockwise) to rotate the crane arms.
  • Hold Space to enable the magnet and pick up the vinyl when the magnet is close to its center.
  • Press Arrow Up Arrow Down to rotate the triangles on the vinyl.
  • Move the mouse in the canvas to move the camera. Scroll to zoom in/out.
  • The music will start playing when the vinyl is closed to the turntable.
